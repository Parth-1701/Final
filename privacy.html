Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Quizable ("we," "our," or "us"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website (quizable.online) and use our educational services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide you with a seamless educational experience. This includes:

  • Account Data: When you register, we collect your email address, name, and securely hashed passwords to create and manage your account.
  • Performance Data: We store your mock test scores, time taken per question, and overall progress to generate your analytics dashboard and percentiles.
  • Transaction Data: If you purchase a premium masterclass, our third-party payment gateways (e.g., Razorpay, PhonePe) collect your payment details. We do not store your credit card, debit card, or UPI PIN details on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly for operational and educational purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Quizable platform.
  • To process transactions and send you related information, including purchase confirmations and invoices.
  • To analyze leaderboard data and improve the accuracy of our percentile predictor.
  • To communicate with you regarding platform updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with trusted third-party services that help us operate our platform:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure user authentication, database hosting, and server infrastructure.
  • Payment Gateways: Used securely to process premium subscription payments.
  • Email Services: Used to send you password resets and important platform notifications.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use standard functional cookies to keep you logged in across different sessions and to save your dark-mode/light-mode UI preferences. We may also use basic analytics to understand which pages (like specific masterclasses or mock tests) are the most popular so we can create more of that content.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption and secure database architecture (via Firebase), to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you. You may update your profile details via your dashboard. If you wish to permanently delete your Quizable account and all associated test data, please contact our support team, and we will process your request within 7 business days.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at:

Email: support@quizable.online
