Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Quizable, we are committed to providing high-quality digital mock tests, video masterclasses, and educational resources to help you succeed in your examinations.

Because our products are purely digital and grant immediate access to proprietary content upon purchase, all sales are final. We maintain a strict No Refund policy.

1. Digital Goods Policy

By purchasing a premium membership, course, or test series on Quizable, you acknowledge that you are purchasing digital content. Once a transaction is successful, access to the materials is granted automatically and instantly. Therefore, we cannot offer refunds, exchanges, or cancellations once a purchase is made.

We highly recommend utilizing our free previews, sample mock tests, and free course lectures to evaluate the quality of our platform before making a premium purchase.

2. Exceptions for Technical Failures

While we do not offer refunds for "change of mind" or "lack of usage," we will issue a full refund under the following specific technical circumstances:

  • Duplicate Transactions: If your bank account or UPI is charged multiple times for the exact same transaction due to a network error.
  • Failed Unlocks: If your payment is successfully deducted by the payment gateway, but the Quizable platform fails to unlock your premium account within 24 hours, and our technical team is unable to resolve the issue.

3. Claiming an Exceptional Refund

If you experience a duplicate charge or failed unlock as described above, you must contact our support team within 3 days of the transaction. Please provide:

  • The email address associated with your Quizable account.
  • The transaction ID or screenshot of the payment receipt.
  • A brief description of the issue.

Approved refunds for technical failures will be credited back to the original method of payment within 5-7 business days, depending on your bank.

4. Cancellations

If you have purchased a recurring subscription (rather than a one-time lifetime pass), you may cancel your subscription at any time through your dashboard. Cancellation will stop future billing, but no prorated refunds will be issued for the current billing cycle. You will retain access to your premium features until the end of your currently paid cycle.
